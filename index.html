How it works

Up and running in seconds.

01
Open the app
No sign-up. No install. Just open TaskFlow in any browser and you're ready to go. Your tasks are saved locally and persist between sessions.
02
Add your tasks
Click "Add Task" to create daily tasks, or switch to Monthly / Annual Goals for longer-term planning. Double-click any item to rename it.
03
Connect Google Calendar (optional)
Head to the Calendar tab and hit "Connect Google Calendar". Your events load alongside your tasks so everything lives in one place.
04
Stay on top of your day
Use the progress bar to see how much you've accomplished. Navigate forward and back through days, months, and years at a glance.
